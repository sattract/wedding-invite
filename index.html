The Sacred Union

Vivaah

शिव बिना शक्ति शून्य है, और शक्ति बिना शिव दिशाहीन। शिव-शक्ति का मिलन ही ब्रह्मांड का मूल सत्य है—जहाँ चेतना और ऊर्जा एक होकर पूर्णता को प्राप्त करते हैं।

Shiva-Shakti

This represents the ultimate, inseparable union. Shiva is pure consciousness (stillness), while Shakti is dynamic energy (movement)

Radha-Krishna

This embodies the highest form of love (Prema) and total devotion, representing the union of the soul with the supreme Lord.

Vishnu & Lakshmi/Tulsi

Represents the union of preservation and prosperity. The Tulsi Vivah signifies the sacred connection between devotion (Tulsi) and the Divine Lord.

Human Marriage as Sacrament

Hindu marriage is considered a sacred union of two souls rather than a social contract. It is a religious duty (dharma) involving rituals like Saptapadi (seven steps) that foster mutual support, fidelity, and partnership
